WHAT ARE YOUR

SUMMER PLANS?

It was a hard year and everyone needs a break this summer. At Fusion Academy, we work with you to create a summer program and schedule that works for you. Don’t sacrifice much-needed rest or vacation for summer learning. We’re here to help you do both.

Whether your child needs to remediate, accelerate, or build key skills for future success, Fusion’s one-to-one programs are personalized for your student. Learn more about all our summer offerings in the following pages or connect with us directly at  855.333.7075.

Remediate

Fusion offers classes for credit and tutoring for students to fill learning loss gaps, recover failed credits, or rebuild a learning foundation.

Accelerate

Advance your learning this summer with a class for credit in a personalized, one-to-one setting that’s taken at your accelerated pace.

Build Key Skills

Sharpen your skills this summer with our unique Skill Launch Camps designed to build necessary skills for student success.

CATCH UP

THIS SUMMER

It was a tough school year for everyone. You and your student need some downtime this summer, but it’s also a valuable time to get back on track. Fusion Academy offers a convenient and safe option to remediate classes or rebuild a solid learning foundation. Fit your learning around vacations, sports, or whatever fun you have planned; Fusion makes both learning and leisure possible.

Remediate with Classes for Credit

Classes at Fusion Academy are one-to- one: one teacher and one student per classroom. This personalized environment is great for students who need to make up a class and truly learn the material before the next school year.

Popular subjects include:

  • Algebra
  • Chemistry
  • English
  • Spanish
  • Calculus
  • Physics
  • Economics
  • U.S. History

Fill Gaps in Learning with Personalized Tutoring

If your child got burnt out this past school year, this is a great way to get them re-engaged in their education. It’s also a great way to build a strong foundation in a tough subject before school starts in the fall.

Popular subjects include:

  • Math
  • World Language
  • Science
  • English
  • Study Skills
  • Executive Functioning
